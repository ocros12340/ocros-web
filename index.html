About

Okros is a Budapest-based sound designer working across film post-production, advertising, and film score composition. The work is built on a single conviction: that sound shapes emotional truth before the mind catches up.

With a background in both technical audio engineering and music composition, Okros brings a dual sensitivity to every project — the precision of the editor and the ear of the composer.

Available for projects worldwide, with experience in both independent and agency-facing workflows.
